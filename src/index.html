How does it work?

Choose a city and quest from list. Read the quest description, it will introduce you to the story. Go to the starting point and start the adventure.
You are the main character who takes part in the quest, your choices will affect a plot like in role playing games. To complete quest you might have to explore town, look for clues, talk to NPCs, fight with enemies or whatever comes to the questwriter's mind. Every quest is unique so put yourself in the role.
We wish you exciting experiences. Good luck.
